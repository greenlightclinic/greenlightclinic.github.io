We Know That Things Can Get Overwhelming

Young people, living in the Bay Area can often experience confusion and conflict around how to deal with their families, peers, school, economics, and work. We are here to help.

"Greenlight Clinic is a free short term, therapy setting for 14 – 25 year olds living in the Bay Area. In a safe environment, young people can learn to identify and manage their feelings around issues with families, peers, school, economics, and work. These feelings can result in feelings of sadness, depression, anger, frustration and anxiety. They can also result in very serious consequences such as suicidal feelings, anger, and risk taking behaviors."

Greenlight Clinic Goals

  • Improve the quality of life for young people
  • Help identify and manage feelings
  • Help communicate feelings
  • Help to navigate through any crisis
  • Help to stabilize through any situation

Greenlight Clinic Process

  • We offer 50-minute therapy sessions
  • Provide free therapy for up to 6 months
  • Provide issue focused and age focused group therapy
  • Work with licensed therapist and experienced interns
  • Provide booster sessions to insure stability and safety
  • Provide referrals to low fee agencies
